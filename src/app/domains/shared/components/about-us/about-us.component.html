About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind our success.

Our Mission

O-Film is a background film collection made and edited by film lovers volunteers in order to give feedback and improve cinematographic industries and make a community with critical analysis where people can make easier decissions about movies.

Our Vision

Our vission is to give a world free access to the first and biggest film gallery recognized around the world and to be a movement for fighting against piracy and also promote the film industries to create truly great movies.

Our Team

We are a community of film lovers located around the world.
